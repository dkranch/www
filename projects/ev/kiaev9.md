Since we were at a Hyundai dealership, we decided to try and drive an EV9. It's a large 3 row car, and it's more than we need, but we were wondering about the Hyundai/Genesis EV experience.

It was nice, and it had leather-ish interior, lots of options, and a $65k price tag.

However, it felt like a nice car, not a luxury one.
